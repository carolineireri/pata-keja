ABOUT US

Pata Keja is a leading residential property company in Kenya, working across local markets, delivering unrivalled customer service through the commitment and inspiration of its staff.The success of our business is founded on the global affiliation and the size of our local network. Listening to the needs of our customers, we saw their need for professional help not only in terms of finding affordable accommodation, but also after-sales. That's why we distinguish from a number of similar agencies; we offer our clients additional services.

Our Vision

At Pata Keja our goal is to go above and beyond to deliver an experience that is fulfilling for our customers. We realize our business is based on relationships and that every individual interaction makes a difference.

We will diligently work with the people we serve and guide them . Because they're people first and clients second.

Our Mission

Pata Keja's mission is to be the most professional and ethical company in Kenya and to positively impact our clients and our community:

  • Being committed to our business full time.
  • Making ourselves available when you are.
  • Working hard to turn clients into our fans.
